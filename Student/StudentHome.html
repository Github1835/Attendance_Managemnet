About

B & B Institute of technology

Bhailalbhai & Bhikhabhai Institute of Technology (Polytechnic) is a grant-in-aid and Self Financed Polytechnic managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal, Vallabh Vidyanagar. The institute was established in August 1958 with the intake capacity of 60 each in conventional programmes like Civil, Mechanical & Electrical Engg. The institute has also started two programmes under Self Finance pattern; in the year 2001, it has started Diploma in Information Technology with 60 intake and in 2003, Diploma in Electronics & Communication with 60 intake.
